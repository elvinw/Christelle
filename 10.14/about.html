ABOUT US

HISTORY

Christelle Ltd., formerly known as Wee Soon Ltd., specializes in the design and creation of fine jewelry in line with the latest trends using a combination of diamond, pearl, and semiprecious stones. With a vertically integrated manufacturing facility and over 3 generations of experience in the Asia Pacific, Europe, and the Americas, we provide a seamless experience in the curation, design, and creation of their own jewelry lines whilst ensuring competitive pricing.

QUALITY

Each piece delivered to our customers should be no less than perfect and we reflect this in our meticulous quality control procedures at every step of our production process. We guarantee the disclosure of any treatments to raw materials used and assure that each of our diamonds are of natural origin from non-conflict zones and do not contain any HPHT or CVD synthetics.

DOING BUSINESS WITH US

We are open to partnering with wholesalers, retailers, designers, and startup brands interested in curating our designs or creating their own custom pieces. We believe in customer anonymity and guarantee that we will not use, produce, sell nor disclose original designs by our customers.

There is no minimum on orders and we are flexible on our customers requirements such as gold finish, gold purity 14K/18K, and diamond quality VS/SI/I. We guarantee that each of our diamonds are of natural origin not contianing any HPHT or CVD synthetics from non-conflict zones. Full disclosure on any treatments on colored stones is always provided.
