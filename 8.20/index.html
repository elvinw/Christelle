ABOUT US

With over 40 years of experience, we provide our customers with a seamless experience in creating their own jewelry lines through the design of their own custom pieces or the curation of our original company designs.

Based in Hong Kong, we partner with various brands throughout the Asia Pacific and Americas in developing their unique jewelry lines.

There is no minimum on orders and we are flexible on requirements such as gold finish, gold purity 14K/18K, and diamond quality VS/SI/I.
